Company: Megastar Financial/Take 3 Technologies

I developed this mobile app from the ground up while working at Take 3 Technologies. This app was created with Xamarin, and customized for both Android and iOS devices. I was in control of the design of the app, and used api calls to access information from the LoanMaps CRM database.

The main purpose of this app (at least its first few iterations) was to allow loan officers to keep in touch with existing contacts (clients and potential leads), and to add new contacts, as well as to allow loan officers to keep track of activities/tasks that needed to be completed.

Company: abas-USA

During my time at abas-USA, I worked on three mobile apps: Receiving, Production, and Inventory. These apps were built using Xamarin forms, and I built the Receiving app from the ground up. A desktop/Windows app was also built using the Xamarin platform.

These mobile apps served the purpose of helping businesses better manage their inventory (such as warehouses). The mobile applications utilized the abas-USA custom CRM, and made the features simpler and more accessible to customers/clients of abas-USA.
